Kubernetes (EKS)
----------------

Situation:
    Monolithic app needed better scalability and fault tolerance.

Action:
    Migrated to microservices on Kubernetes (EKS), used Deployments, Services, HPA, and Ingress (ALB).

Result:

    Achieved auto-scaling based on load
    Improved availability across AZs
    Zero-downtime deployments

Use case line:
    “Used Kubernetes for orchestration, auto-scaling, and self-healing of containerized apps.”
