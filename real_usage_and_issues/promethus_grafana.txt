Prometheus + Grafana

👉 Situation:
Needed detailed application and infra-level metrics.

👉 Action:

Prometheus for scraping metrics
Grafana for dashboards
Configured alerts (CPU, pod restarts, latency)

👉 Result:

Deep visibility into system performance
Faster troubleshooting
Data-driven scaling decisions

👉 Use case line:
“Used Prometheus and Grafana for real-time monitoring and alerting of Kubernetes workloads.”
